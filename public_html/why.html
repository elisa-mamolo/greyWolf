WHY CHOOSING ECO?

Grey Wolf is a new startup company that is hitting the market with cool eco minimal clothes. Ecological clothes are good for the environment and for people healthy too. Buying eco you'll help saving a lot of water that is usually wasted during clothes production, making this choice you'll be part of a movement that is spreading around the world. "Buy eco and spread the echo", this is our slogan, Grey Wolf is a quality, trustworthy brand that want to achieve the goal of saving the environment while wearing stylish clothes.

ICON FABRICS

We have two fabric collection. Organic cotton and bamboo are both grown without toxic chemicals. Organic cotton doesn’t damage the soil, has less impact on the air, and uses 71% less water and 62% less energy. Conventional cotton uses about 16% of the world’s insecticides and 7% of pesticides and it has less quality than the organic one.
Bamboo fiber naturally resists microbes (bacteria & fungus) and is sustainable & eco-friendly. It requires 1/3 of the amount of water used for regular cotton production.
